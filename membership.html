Who We Are

A Volunteer Organization

KVBA is a volunteer organization dedicated to promoting blues music in the Kalamazoo Valley and surrounding area. Our members are people just like you who love blues music and want to see it continue to grow and thrive in our community.

KVBA hosts numerous events throughout the year including the annual Kalamazoo Blues Festival, the Winter Blues Series, the Blues in Schools program, the Boogie Woogie Bob Peters Scholarship, and is a member of the Blues Foundation, which hosts the International Blues Challenge (IBC) in Memphis each January.

We also support and promote the Blues Challenge, held each fall, where Kalamazoo-area bands and solo/duo artists compete to represent KVBA at the IBC.

What Your Membership Funds

🎪

Kalamazoo Blues Festival

KVBA hosts the annual Kalamazoo Blues Festival — a free, community-wide celebration bringing regional and national blues talent to the streets of Kalamazoo.

🎓

Blues in Schools

Our education program brings professional blues musicians into local schools, teaching students about the history, roots, and culture of this foundational American music.

🏆

Scholarship Fund

The Boogie Woogie Bob Peters Scholarship supports young musicians pursuing blues music. Every membership contributes directly to this fund.

🎸

Blues Challenge & IBC

KVBA is a member of the Blues Foundation and hosts the annual Blues Challenge, sending Kalamazoo's best to compete at the International Blues Challenge in Memphis each January.
