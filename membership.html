What Your Membership Funds

🎸

Live Events

KVBA helps bring world-class and local blues artists to Kalamazoo Valley venues all year long, from intimate jams to the annual Blues Festival.

🎓

Blues in Schools

Our education program brings professional blues musicians into local schools, teaching students about the history and culture of the blues.

🏆

Scholarships

The Boogie Woogie Bob Peters Scholarship supports young musicians pursuing blues music. Every membership contributes directly to this fund.

🌍

Preservation

We work to document and preserve the rich tradition of the blues — honoring its roots while fostering the next generation of blues artists.
