Schedule of Events

12:30 PM

Registration & Warm-Up

Check in, collect your cart, and warm up on the range. Range balls provided. Lunch available on your own at the clubhouse.

2:00 PM

Shotgun Tee Off

Scramble format — teams of four. All skill levels welcome. Live blues music on the course throughout the round.

7:00 PM

Dinner & Awards

Dinner served in the clubhouse followed by the awarding of golf prizes and raffle prizes. A great way to close a great day.
