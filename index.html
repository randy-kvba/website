Who We Are

The Heart of the Blues in Kalamazoo

The Kalamazoo Valley Blues Association has been the heartbeat of blues music in Southwest Michigan for decades. We're a community of musicians, fans, and supporters united by our love of the blues.

From intimate jams at local bars to the annual Kalamazoo Blues Festival, KVBA connects the community to the music that moves us. We also invest in the future through our Blues in Schools education program and the Boogie Woogie Bob Peters Scholarship.
