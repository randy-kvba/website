Support the Blues

Become a Sponsor

Help keep live blues music alive in Southwest Michigan. Your sponsorship supports the festival, education programs, and the local blues community.

Why Sponsor

31 Years of Blues in Kalamazoo

Celebrating 31 years of bringing the blues to the zoo, it's time to get back to where it all started! Join us as we celebrate this great American art form on July 18–19, 2025 at Arcadia Creek Festival Site. There are several sponsorship opportunities listed below, followed by purchase links at the bottom of this page.

Sponsoring KVBA puts your brand in front of thousands of blues fans and aligns your business with 31 years of community investment. From stage banners to program listings, we'll make sure your support is seen and appreciated.
